Thank you for Connecting with the Victory Seventh-day Adventist Church via our website. We hope your online experience provides you a great taste of worship and fellowship at Victory. No matter where you are or what season of life you are in, you are welcome to join us in worship and fellowship.

We are located in the North Bronx area of the borough of Bronx, one of the five boroughs of our great city, New York City; the city we love and serve. We are excited about how God is changing lives because of our church, and we would love for you to be part of the awesome change!

You're welcome to visit this site day or night. If you're ever in the North Bronx area please stop by Victory to meet us face to face and experience the full worship and fellowship at victorious Victory! Welcome again!
